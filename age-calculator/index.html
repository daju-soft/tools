How to Calculate Age

Age is calculated by subtracting the date of birth from the current date. The result gives the number of complete years, months, and days that have passed. This tool also calculates the total number of days, weeks, hours, and minutes lived which can be a surprising and interesting perspective on time.

Why Age Calculations Are Tricky

Age calculation seems simple but months have different lengths and leap years add an extra day every four years. A person born on February 29 only has a birthday on the actual date once every four years. This tool handles all these edge cases correctly including leap year birthdays and month-end date differences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I calculate age at a past or future date?

Yes. Change the Calculate Age At field to any date in the past or future. This is useful for calculating how old someone was on a specific date or will be on a future date.

Is my date of birth stored?

No. Everything runs in your browser. No data is sent to any server or stored anywhere.
