How to Calculate Percentages

A percentage is a number expressed as a fraction of 100. The word percent comes from the Latin per centum meaning by the hundred. To find X percent of a number, multiply the number by X divided by 100. To find what percentage X is of Y, divide X by Y and multiply by 100.

Percentage Change

Percentage change shows how much a value has increased or decreased relative to its original value. The formula is the new value minus the old value, divided by the old value, multiplied by 100. A positive result means an increase. A negative result means a decrease.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between percentage and percentage points?

Percentage is relative. If something grows from 10 percent to 15 percent, it grew by 50 percent (5 is 50 percent of 10). Percentage points are absolute. The same change is 5 percentage points. The distinction matters a lot in financial and statistical reporting.
