What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem ipsum is placeholder text used in design and publishing. It has been the industry standard dummy text since the 1500s. The text comes from a work by Cicero but is scrambled so it reads as meaningless Latin. Designers use it to fill space in mockups and wireframes without distracting viewers with readable content.

Using real content in a design mockup draws attention to the words rather than the layout. Lorem ipsum lets reviewers focus on fonts, spacing, and visual hierarchy instead of reading the text. It is one of the most widely used tools in graphic design and web development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does lorem ipsum come from?

It comes from sections of a work by Cicero called "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" written in 45 BC. The text has been scrambled and altered over the centuries and is no longer recognisable as the original Latin.

Can I use this text in a real project?

Yes for mockups and prototypes. Never ship a real product with lorem ipsum still in it. Always replace placeholder text with real content before going live.

Is my text saved anywhere?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
